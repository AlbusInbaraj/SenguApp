Thirukural Daily

Tirukkural, (Tamil: “Sacred Couplets”), also called Kural, the most celebrated of the Patiren-kirkkanakku (“Eighteen Ethical Works”) in Tamil literature and a work that has had an immense influence on Tamil culture and life. It is usually attributed to the poet Tiruvalluvar, who is thought to have lived in India in the 6th century, though some scholars assign an earlier date (1st century BC). In its practical concerns, aphoristic insights into daily life, and universal and timeless approach, the Tirukural has been compared to the great books of the world’s major religions.
